# Data Privacy Questions

## Data Collection

### Q1: What data does the platform collect?

**A**: CogniAND platform collects the following types of data:

**Account Information:**
- Email address, name, gender, date of birth
- User role (subject/experimenter)

**Experiment Data:**
- Experiment response times, choice and judgment results
- Task completion status, experiment progress records

**Usage Data:**
- Login time and frequency, platform usage behavior
- Experiment participation records, system interaction logs

We follow the **data minimization principle**, collecting only necessary data and not collecting personal information unrelated to experiments.

Details: [Data Collection](/en/5-data-privacy/1-data-collection)

### Q2: How long is data retained?

**A**: Retention periods for different types of data:

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account data | Duration of account existence |
| Experiment data | According to research requirements (typically 5-10 years) |
| Usage logs | 6 months |
| Deleted account data | Permanently deleted within 30 days |

Details: [Data Collection](/en/5-data-privacy/1-data-collection)

## Privacy Protection

### Q3: How does the platform protect my privacy?

**A**: We employ multiple protection measures:

1. **Data Anonymization**
   - Experiment data identified by unique IDs
   - No linkage to personal identity information
   - Automatic anonymization on data export

2. **Access Control**
   - Strict permission management
   - Secure data storage
   - Limited data usage scope

3. **User Control**
   - Set privacy preferences
   - Control data sharing scope
   - Delete account at any time

Details: [Privacy Protection](/en/5-data-privacy/2-privacy-protection)

### Q4: Will my data be shared with third parties?

**A**: We **will not sell, rent, or trade** your personal information. Data is only shared in the following situations:

- ✅ With your explicit consent
- ✅ Required by laws and regulations
- ✅ Academic collaboration (anonymized data)
- ✅ Service providers (bound by confidentiality agreements)

All third-party services we use (email services, cloud storage, analytics tools) have signed data protection agreements.

Details: [Privacy Protection](/en/5-data-privacy/2-privacy-protection)

## Informed Consent

### Q5: What is informed consent? Why is it important?

**A**: Informed consent is an important step before participating in experiments, ensuring you fully understand the experiment content and participate voluntarily.

**Informed consent includes:**
- Experiment information (purpose, process, duration, requirements)
- Data usage (collection types, usage methods, retention period)
- Participant rights (voluntary participation, withdrawal at any time, data access)
- Risks and benefits (possible discomfort, potential benefits, compensation details)
- Contact information (researchers, ethics committee, complaint channels)

**Informed consent process:**
1. Read the informed consent form
2. Liveness detection (verify real person)
3. Confirm consent
4. Start experiment

Details: [Informed Consent](/en/5-data-privacy/3-informed-consent)

### Q6: Can I withdraw consent after agreeing?

**A**: Yes, you have the right to withdraw consent at any time:

- ✅ Exit ongoing experiments
- ✅ Request deletion of collected data
- ⚠️ Does not affect completed experiment data (already used for research)

**How to withdraw:**
- Click "Exit Experiment" in the experiment interface
- Contact researchers or platform administrators
- Submit a request through [Technical Support](/en/7-technical-support/1-contact)

Details: [Informed Consent](/en/5-data-privacy/3-informed-consent)

## Ethics Standards

### Q7: What ethical principles does the platform follow?

**A**: CogniAND platform strictly adheres to psychological research ethics standards:

**Four core principles:**

1. **Respect Principle**
   - Respect participants' autonomy and privacy
   - Respect cultural differences, treat all participants equally

2. **Beneficence Principle**
   - Maximize research benefits, minimize potential risks
   - Provide necessary support, share research results

3. **Justice Principle**
   - Fair selection of participants, reasonable distribution of research burdens and benefits
   - Avoid discrimination and bias, protect vulnerable groups' rights

4. **Integrity Principle**
   - Truthfully report research results, avoid academic misconduct
   - Maintain research transparency, use data responsibly

Details: [Ethics Standards](/en/5-data-privacy/4-ethics)

### Q8: How are experiments ethically reviewed?

**A**: All experiments must undergo ethical review:

**Review process:**
1. Researchers submit experiment protocol
2. Ethics committee reviews
3. Assess risks and benefits
4. Provide modification suggestions
5. Approve or reject

**Review criteria:**
- Scientific validity of research design
- Adequacy of informed consent
- Reasonableness of risk assessment
- Effectiveness of privacy protection
- Standardization of data management

**Special group protection:**
- Minors require guardian consent
- Vulnerable groups require special ethical review and enhanced protection measures

Details: [Ethics Standards](/en/5-data-privacy/4-ethics)

## User Rights

### Q9: What data rights do I have?

**A**: You have the following data rights:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| ✅ Data access right | View your own data |
| ✅ Data modification right | Modify personal information |
| ✅ Data deletion right | Request account deletion |
| ✅ Data export right | Export your own data |
| ✅ Right to refuse | Refuse specific data collection |
| ✅ Right to withdraw | Withdraw informed consent |

**How to exercise rights:**
- Log in to personal center to view and modify data
- Contact [Technical Support](/en/7-technical-support/1-contact) to request deletion or export
- Withdraw consent in the experiment interface

Details: [Data & Privacy](/en/5-data-privacy/)

### Q10: How do I delete my data?

**A**: You can delete data through the following methods:

**Delete account:**
1. Contact platform administrator to request account deletion
2. Provide account information for identity verification
3. Confirm deletion operation (irreversible)
4. All personal data permanently deleted within 30 days

**Delete experiment data:**
- Can exit at any time during experiments and request data deletion
- Completed experiment data may be retained for research purposes (anonymized)

**Important notes:**
- ⚠️ Deletion operation is irreversible, please proceed with caution
- ⚠️ Anonymized data already used for scientific research may not be deletable
- ⚠️ Account cannot be recovered after deletion

Contact: [Technical Support](/en/7-technical-support/1-contact)
