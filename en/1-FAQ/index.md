## Most Common Questions

### How to register an account?

Click the "Login/Register" button in the top right corner, select the "Register" tab, fill in email, password, name and other information, select role (subject/experimenter), submit and the system will send a verification email. Click the verification link in the email to complete registration.

For detailed steps, see: [Subject Manual - Registration](/en/3-subject-manual/2-registration-login/2-1-register)

### Forgot password?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, enter your registered email, and the system will send a password reset email. Click the link in the email to set a new password.

For detailed steps, see: [Subject Manual - Password Reset](/en/3-subject-manual/2-registration-login/2-4-password-reset)

### How to participate in experiments?

Log in to your subject account, browse the experiment list on the homepage, click on an experiment you're interested in to view details, and click the "Apply" button (⚠️ currently a placeholder feature).

For detailed steps, see: [Subject Manual - Participate in Experiments](/en/3-subject-manual/4-participate-experiment/)
